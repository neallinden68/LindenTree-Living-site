Student Housing, Made Simple

LindenTree Living was founded by a University of Illinois alum who saw firsthand how stressful, expensive, and unfair student housing could be. After graduating, we set out to make renting easier, more transparent, and a lot less of a headache for students and young renters in Champaign-Urbana. Our mission is simple: provide housing that’s straightforward, fairly priced, and well-managed — no hidden fees, no confusing processes, and no getting lost in the shuffle. We’re a small company, which means we can focus on what matters most: treating every renter with respect and making sure they feel at home. Whether you’re a student looking for a place near campus or just someone searching for a comfortable space in town, we’re here to make the process clear and stress-free.

Why Students Choose Us

  • Affordable & Fair: No hidden fees or suprises charges.
  • Fast Communication: Same day responses guranteed.
  • Close to Campus: Walkable and bikeable locations for an easy commute.
  • Reliable Maintenance: Quick fixes when something breaks.
